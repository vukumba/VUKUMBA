About VUKUMBA (Pty) Ltd

Founded in 2021 by Vutlharhi Phakula, VUKUMBA (Pty) Ltd is a 100% Black Economic Empowerment company committed to fostering economic growth and empowerment. Our mission is to alleviate poverty, create jobs, and support women, youth, and disabled individuals through sustainable business initiatives.

As a multi-division company, we specialize in providing high-quality farming and industrial equipment, stationery, computer peripherals, and other essential supplies. We are dedicated to maintaining excellence, respect, and integrity in all our services.
